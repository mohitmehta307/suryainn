Our Story

A Sanctuary Cradled
by the Himalayas

Nestled in the gentle folds of the Kumaon Himalayas, Surya Inn is more than a place to stay — it's a place to breathe, to reconnect, and to remember what silence sounds like.

Perched above the shimmering Bhimtal Lake at 1,500 metres on the Bhimtal–Haldwani Highway, our mid-valley retreat offers an intimate escape from the noise of the plains. Wake to the soft call of Himalayan birds, sip morning chai as mist rolls off pine-draped ridges, and let the rhythm of mountain life restore you.

Beyond the Room

Experiences at Surya Inn

Bhimtal is nature's gift — and we make sure you experience every inch of it.

Bhimtal Lake Boating

Glide across the serene waters of Bhimtal Lake, one of the most beautiful high-altitude lakes in Uttarakhand. Visit the iconic island aquarium in the middle of the lake.

Forest Trails & Treks

Explore ancient oak and rhododendron forests on guided trails. Routes for all fitness levels — from gentle morning walks to demanding ridge hikes with Himalayan panoramas.

Sunrise Photography

Our rooftop terrace offers some of the finest sunrise vantage points in Kumaon. Golden light spilling over pine-covered ridges — a photographer's paradise every morning.

Evening Bonfire

As the Himalayan night settles in, gather around our bonfire under a canopy of stars. Sip kahwa, share stories, and feel the warmth beneath a sky undimmed by city lights.

Musical Nights

Enjoy live music sessions under the open Himalayan sky. Whether it's folk Kumaoni tunes or DJ nights, the mountains set the perfect stage for an unforgettable evening.

Bird Watching

Bhimtal is a birder's haven. With over 200 species including the Himalayan Bulbul, Verditer Flycatcher, and the elusive Koklass Pheasant — every walk is a discovery.
